About Me

Hello, my Name is Mirza. I share a deep passion in just about anything related to computers and technology including (but not limited to) hardware/software, programming, video games, and some interest in CG art. I was born and raised here in Canada alongside my two younger sisters, but the rest of my family was born and raised in Pakistan.

Programming has caught my interest since third grade, when I found a JavaScript fundamentals course on Khan Academy and decided to teach myself it, and I had a computer at a very young age which honed my technical skills. Even with all of this knowledge absorbed, I feel like I do not know enough; that's why I want to learn a little bit of everything, including web design, and decide a field from there.
